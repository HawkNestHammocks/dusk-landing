CONCEPT — FOR PROTOTYPING

Portable Steam Sauna — Stove-Mounted Steam Generator

Sealed internal-flame boiler turns canister fuel into steam, piped into a reflective pop-up tent. No rocks. No CO inside.

★ KEY SAFETY / DIFFERENTIATOR

Combustion & exhaust stay OUTSIDE the tent. Only clean, dry steam is piped in — eliminating carbon-monoxide risk and the exploding-rock hazard entirely.
