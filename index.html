Questions

Good. Ask the hard ones.

Is it really melatonin-free?

Completely. MOONSET works with magnesium glycinate, L-theanine, glycine, ashwagandha, saffron and apigenin (from chamomile) — nutrients, aminos and adaptogens your body already uses to wind down. No added hormones, so nothing for your body to become reliant on.

Isn't chocolate a stimulant?

Real cacao is — it hides caffeine and a longer-acting stimulant called theobromine, which is exactly why hot chocolate can keep sensitive people up. MOONSET is built on carob instead: rich dark-chocolate flavour, zero caffeine, zero theobromine.

Will it knock me out?

No — and that's the point. It doesn't sedate you; it lowers the noise so your body can do what it already knows how to do. You ease into sleep and wake clear, not foggy.

When and how do I take it?

Stir one scoop into 8oz of warm or cold milk (dairy or plant) about 30 minutes before bed.

What does it taste like?

A rich, lightly-spiced cocoa — a cozy, grown-up hot chocolate. Naturally sweetened with monk fruit, no added sugar.
